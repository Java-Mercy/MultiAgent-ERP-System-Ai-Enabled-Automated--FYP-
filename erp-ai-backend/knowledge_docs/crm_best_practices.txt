CRM Best Practices — Operational Standards
===========================================

DATA HYGIENE
- Always log every interaction immediately — not at end of day.
- Update lead stage after each contact or meaningful event.
- Add timestamped notes with key discussion points after every call, meeting, or email.
- Verify and correct contact information (email, phone, company name) at first interaction.
- Remove or merge duplicate leads within 24 hours of detection.

LEAD STAGES (Odoo Pipeline)
1. New — Lead just created, not yet contacted.
2. Qualified — Initial contact made, need confirmed.
3. Proposition — Proposal or demo delivered.
4. Won — Deal closed successfully.
5. Lost — Deal closed unsuccessfully. Always record the loss reason.

STAGE PROGRESSION RULES
- Never skip a stage without documenting why.
- Move a lead to "Lost" instead of leaving it stagnant when there is no realistic path forward.
- A lead should not stay in "New" for more than 48 hours after creation.

COMMUNICATION STANDARDS
- All outbound emails must be logged in the lead's chatter/notes.
- Subject lines must be specific — never use "Following up" alone.
- CC the assigned sales rep on all AI-generated emails before sending.
- Never send bulk emails from a lead record — use marketing campaigns instead.

PIPELINE REVIEW
- Every sales rep reviews their pipeline weekly and updates stages.
- Leads with expected close date in the past must be updated or moved to Lost.
- The pipeline meeting happens every Monday at 9 AM — reps must have Odoo updated by Sunday evening.

PRIVACY AND COMPLIANCE
- Never store sensitive personal data (passport, financial details) in lead description fields.
- Respect opt-out requests immediately — mark the contact as "Do Not Contact" in Odoo.
- All customer data is confidential — do not share outside the sales team without manager approval.

AI ASSISTANT USAGE
- AI-generated email drafts must be reviewed by a human before sending.
- AI priority scores are recommendations — the assigned rep has final authority.
- Always verify AI-retrieved lead data against Odoo before acting on it.
- Log when an AI action was taken on a lead (e.g., "AI generated email draft on [date]").
