Follow-Up Guidelines — CRM Sales Process
==========================================

CORE PRINCIPLE
Consistent follow-up is the single biggest driver of closed deals. Most sales are made on the 5th to 8th contact. Never assume silence means disinterest.

TIMING RULES BY PRIORITY

High Priority Leads:
- First follow-up: Within 24 hours of initial contact or lead creation.
- Second follow-up: 48 hours after if no response.
- Third follow-up: 3 days after second.
- Escalate to sales manager if no response after 3 attempts within 1 week.

Medium Priority Leads:
- First follow-up: Within 3 business days.
- Second follow-up: 1 week after if no response.
- Third follow-up: 2 weeks after second.
- Archive lead after 3 unanswered follow-ups over 30 days.

Low Priority Leads:
- First follow-up: Within 1 week.
- Second follow-up: 2 weeks after if no response.
- Move to nurture campaign after 2 unanswered follow-ups.

PERSONALISATION REQUIREMENTS
- Always reference the specific product, service, or problem they mentioned.
- If a previous call or meeting happened, summarise the key takeaway from it.
- Mention any company news or industry event relevant to their business if known.
- Never send a generic "just checking in" email — always add value.

CHANNEL SELECTION
- Email: Default channel for all follow-ups.
- Phone: Use for High priority leads after second unanswered email.
- LinkedIn/Social: Acceptable for re-engagement after 2 failed email attempts, never for first contact.

NOTES AND LOGGING
- Log every follow-up attempt in the CRM immediately after sending.
- Record the channel used, the date, and a brief note on what was sent or said.
- Update the lead stage in Odoo after each meaningful interaction.
- If a lead responds negatively or asks to be removed, update status to "Lost" and note the reason.

HANDOFF PROTOCOL
- If a sales rep is unavailable, another rep may follow up but must log the action under the original rep's name.
- Any lead that goes 14 days without any contact must be flagged for review in the weekly pipeline meeting.
